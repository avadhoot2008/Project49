Interesting Facts About Camels

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  • Thanks to thick pads of skin on their chest and knees, camels can comfortably sit in very hot sand.

  • THEY COME IN A LOT OF DIFFRENT SHAPES AND SIZES
  • THEY HAVE AN EXTREMELY STRONG TOUNGE

Interesting Facts About Cicada

  • Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans
  • There are more than 3000 species of Cicadas in the World
  • There Wings repell water and Bacteria
  • There is a whole enigmatic organization named after them known as the Cicada 3301

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  • THEY MIGHT BE THE FIRST DOMESTICATED BIRD.
  • THEY UNDERSTAND SPACE AND TIME.
  • THEY CAN FIND THEIR WAY BACK TO THE NEST FROM 1300 MILES AWAY.(2092.147 km)
  • THEY SAVED THOUSANDS OF HUMAN LIVES DURING WORLD WARS I AND II.

Interesting Facts About Bears
